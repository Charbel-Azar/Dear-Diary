Our Story


Before Dear Diary Media, we were just four friends who had recently graduated from university, each with promising jobs lined up. Despite this, we all felt there was somehting more waiting for us, a bigger purpose beyond the traditional career path. What set us apart was our diverse skillset, especially in digital marketing. Rather than letting go of our dreams and aspirations, we decided to team up, working on various e-commerce brands and projects. Through persistence and creativity, Dear Diary Media was born, our opportunity to replicate the proven strategies we had developed to help online businesses grow. After three years and partnerships with over 120 local brands, we’ve faced countless challenges, some we never could have anticipated, but we’ve always found innovative ways to overcome them. Looking back, we would make the same choice a hundred times over. It was the brave choice, and that’s what our brand stands for today.
