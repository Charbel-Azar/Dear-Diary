Our story


Dear Diary Media has a very humble beginning in Beirut, Lebanon. We started as a group of 4 friends who were just graduating from college and had already been accepted to very respectable jobs. Being very close, and having a very diverse skillset, we thought of what a waste it would be to take all of our dreams and throw them in the garbage just to accept barely living. There has to be some action, some big problems, some highs and lows, no? That's when we came up with DearDiary Media. We had already made a lot of money through our ecommerce brands, but it was not life changing. So we thought with our diverse skillset, why not replicate our proven strategies to other business all around the world. We all have a passion for the finer things in life, for deeper experiences, for intriguing stories, and what better way is there to turn our wildest imaginations into realities than taking the brave choice and actually doing it. 3 years later, with more than 30 employees and after more than 120 local brands working with us, trust us when we say there isn't a day that doesn't come where we don't have problems we would've never thought of, and we find the most creative ways of solving them. We made this choice, and we would do it 100 times over, the brave choice, and that's what our brand represents now.
